## İngilizce Açıklama

RingLock is an addictive arcade game that tests your reflexes. Catch the shrinking ring at exactly the right moment!

Simple to learn, hard to master: as the pink ring shrinks toward the target, tap the screen to score. The more precise your timing, the higher your score!

**Features:**
• 6 game modes — Classic, Hardcore, Zen, Speed Rush, Mirror, Twin
• Mirror Mode — the ring GROWS instead of shrinking, reversed mechanic!
• Twin Mode — 2 rings at once, chance for double points!
• 22 unique achievements to unlock
• Combo system with precision grading (Perfect / Good / Late)
• 6 ring color themes + 5 background themes
• Daily challenge & streak tracking
• Smooth 60FPS neon animations
• Haptic feedback
• Large text & high contrast accessibility options
• Score sharing
• Fully offline
