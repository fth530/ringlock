RingLock - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

RingLock ("the App") is a timing game developed by Corut. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

RingLock does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game data — including scores, settings, achievements, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Advertising

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information (such as advertising ID) to serve relevant ads. We have configured AdMob with child-safe settings:

  • Ad content rating: General audiences (G)
  • Child-directed treatment: Enabled
  • Non-personalized ads when tracking is declined

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS, the App requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any tracking for ad personalization. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown. Your choice does not affect gameplay.

Children's Privacy

RingLock is rated 9+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad content is filtered for child safety.

Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally using on-device storage. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all saved data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
